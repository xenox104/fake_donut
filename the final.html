Server Rules

1. General Conduct

1.1 Treat all players with respect. Harassment, bullying, or hate speech of any kind will result in an immediate ban.
1.2 No racism, sexism, homophobia, or discrimination of any kind.
1.3 Do not spam or flood the chat.
1.4 English only in public chat. Use DMs for other languages.
1.5 Do not impersonate staff members or other players.

2. Gameplay

2.1 No hacking, cheating, or using unapproved mods/clients. Allowed mods: OptiFine, Sodium, Iris, minimaps (no cave maps).
2.2 No exploiting duplication glitches. Report any bugs you find.
2.3 Griefing outside of designated PvP zones is strictly prohibited.
2.4 Stealing from other players is not allowed unless within PvP zones.
2.5 No spawn camping or repeatedly killing the same player.

3. Builds & Claims

3.1 Do not build within 200 blocks of another player's base without permission.
3.2 Lag machines and excessive redstone contraptions are prohibited.
3.3 Do not build offensive or inappropriate structures.
3.4 Abandoned builds may be removed after 30 days of inactivity.

4. Communication

4.1 No advertising other servers or services.
4.2 No sharing personal information of other players.
4.3 Keep topics in their appropriate Discord channels.
4.4 Do not DM staff unsolicited. Use the proper support channels.
